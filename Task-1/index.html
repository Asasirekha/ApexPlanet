Simple dishes you can make at home

Garlic Butter Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Sauté garlic in butter.
  3. Mix pasta with garlic butter.
  4. Serve hot.

Berry Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Honey (optional)

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients in blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glass and enjoy.

Healthy Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber (Chopped)
  • 1 tomato(Chopped)
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • ½ lemon (for juice)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables: cucumber, tomato, onion, and greens.
  2. Place in a bowl and mix them well.
  3. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss everything together and serve fresh.

Fruit chaat

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple (diced)
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Chop the fruits and place them in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add pomegranate seeds and gently mix the fruits together.
  3. prinkle chaat masala, black salt, and drizzle honey over the fruits.
  4. Toss everything together and garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  5. Serve immediately!
